Our Story

Our founder needed a job and ended up working as an insurance salesman for one of the local agencies. After a while he realized that agency wasn't doing everythhing it could for it's customers, and was taking a major chunk of his commission. Being the honest and moral person he is, he decided to launch his own agency and provide the Las Vegas area with the service and value they deserved. Just like that E-Z Auto Insurance was born.
